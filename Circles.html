Artist statement

A few years ago, I used to believe that emotions-at some point- cease to exist, just like everything else.
We grieve, yet we are able to let go. One moment we are anxious, the next we cannot remember what concerned us. The feeling either faded away or took a different form.
However, I am certain it never remains unchanged. I believe that's how we are hardwired as humans; as much as we appreciate consistency we refuse stillness, and whether we like it or not we outgrow things and feelings.
Ironically enough, here I am, years later, and after all I have been through, I find myself looking for answers to the same questions, as if some feelings lingered... I can still feel them, I talk about them and I try to express them
This make me wonder...
Do Feelings ever come to an end? Or do we withhold them at our disposal to reuse someday? Do we ever move on? Do we run in circles?
does this mean we have always been in the same spot...
that we never moved? Do feelings fade away as time passes
Do we rebuild our scenarios and replicate our feelings in a variety or ways at different stages or our livers?
I am often curious about whether we begin to experience our sentimental odyssey, in a way or a pattern, that shapes how we experience our emotions. What is I want to experience feelings differently?
This is why this project, first and foremost, is a personal challenge, through which I pose questions regarding the VR technology: Is it a technology devoid of sentiments? Can we deliver spirtual messages through it? People might disagree, even argue about the questions aforementioned, however, we can all agree on the fact that technology is to take over the future. What is to become of us shall we not be able to express our genuines selves? In this foreseen future? Will that technology-run world render us emotionless?
