Circles - by Sarah Ayman

  1. the user experiences different emotions stimulated from the surrounding environment and sound effects

    "Circles" was selected to participate in Fourth edition of Art Cairo at the Grand Egyptian Museum



My Contributions

  1. helped the artist make her 3D models come to live by providing scripts that help her combine different actions and events easily from the inspector without needing to code it herself.

  2. provided technical support in dealing with Unity engine, while making the experience

  3. helped in: animations, blender modelling, textures and uv mapping, unity lightining



Artist statement

  1. "The project questions the VR technology: Is it a technology devoid of sentiments? Can we deliver spiritual messages through it? People might disagree, even argue, about the questions aforementioned, however, we can all agree on the fact that technology is to take over the future. What is to become of us shall we not be able to express our genuine selves in this foreseen future? Will that technology-run world render us emotionless?"
